Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy is to explain how Ultralight and its website (collectively, “services”) collect, store, and use the information collected. We respect the user's privacy and are committed to protecting the user’s information.

Information Collection and Use

Ultralight collects anonymous analytics information. This information is generic in nature and cannot be used to individually identify a user. Non-personal information may include technical information about your device, such as your browser type, screen resolution, device type, language, type of operating system, region, and generic information about the use of the application, such as aggregated usage rates of filters and tools. To provide advertisements within the application the third party advertisement services may collect the mobile advertising identifier. Information is gathered by third party services while you are accessing the service. We encourage you to review the policies of the third party operators listed below.

Ultralight uses the information to conduct research and analysis. We do this to improve our services in future updates or to gain knowledge about how our users interact with the service.
